AnaSafe Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

AnaSafe ("the App") is a private anesthesia case logbook. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and optional hospital name — used for authentication and personalization.
  • Case data: Patient case records you create, including clinical details, medications, and notes — stored securely in your private account.
  • Photos: Images you attach to cases — uploaded to secure cloud storage linked to your account.
  • Profile photo: Optional avatar image you upload.

We do NOT collect: device identifiers, location data, analytics, advertising data, browsing history, or any data from other apps on your device.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the case logbook, calculator, and AI assistant features
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data
  • To sync your data across sessions

We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, analytics, or any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS), encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS. Each user's data is isolated using Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own cases and profile.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Google Gemini API: Powers the AnaBot AI assistant. Your chat messages are sent to Google's API for processing. Google's privacy policy applies to this data.
  • EmailJS: Used to send feedback emails when you submit a bug report or feature request.

We do NOT use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only shared with the service providers listed above to deliver app functionality.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time.
  • Export: Export your cases as CSV or JSON from Settings.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings. This is permanent and irreversible.

PDPL Compliance

This app complies with the Saudi Arabia Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). Your data is processed lawfully, transparently, and for specified purposes only.

Children's Privacy

AnaSafe is intended for licensed healthcare professionals and medical students. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17.
